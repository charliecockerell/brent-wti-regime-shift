# Video script — 2 minutes

**Target length:** 280–320 spoken words (2:00 hard cap)
**Format:** Screen recording over notebooks + the money plot
**Target record:** Day 21 (~14 June 2026)

## Storyboard

| Time | Visual | Spoken |
|------|--------|--------|
| 0:00–0:20 | Title card → Brent–WTI spread time series with 2026 spike highlighted | Hook + thesis: spread is cointegrated, shocks cause transient blow-outs, 2026 may be structural. |
| 0:20–0:50 | `01_cointegration.ipynb` — ADF + β + ECM half-life output | Cointegration setup: β value, half-life of mean reversion in days. |
| 0:50–1:20 | Money plot — stacked spread-z paths | Event study: historical shocks show consistent mean-reversion timescale; show the median + IQR. |
| 1:20–1:50 | OOS PnL curve | Trade rule + backtest result: Sharpe, drawdown, when it fails honestly. |
| 1:50–2:00 | Rolling-β chart with post-2026 overlay | Long-term repricing: regime may be shifting; close with the limitation. |

## Script draft

> Filled in during Phase 7. Read aloud against a stopwatch — written words come out longer than they read. Aim for the bottom of the 280–320 range.

## Recording notes

- One take per section, edit in-line.
- Zoom in on numbers — viewer can't pause a competition submission.
- Quiet room. AirPods mic is fine; don't over-engineer.
- Export 1080p MP4. No music.

## Quant-signal flag

If the paper is taking longer than expected at Day 19, ship a shorter, lower-production-value video before sacrificing paper quality. A strong paper with an OK video beats a weak paper with a polished one.
