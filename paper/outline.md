# Paper outline — 1,000 words

**Title:** The Brent–WTI Spread Under Middle East Supply Shocks: Mean Reversion, Regime Drift, and the 2026 Hormuz Crisis
**Submission:** Onyx Research Competition 2026 — Technical Track
**Target length:** 1,000 words (hard cap)
**Target ship:** 23 June 2026

## Section budget

| Section | Words | Content |
|---------|-------|---------|
| Thesis | 50 | One paragraph: spread is cointegrated; shocks cause transient blow-outs; 2026 may be a structural break. |
| Spread regime + cointegration | 200 | ADF results, Engle-Granger β with CI, Johansen rank, ECM half-life. Table of stats. |
| Event study | 250 | Per-event metrics table (Aramco 2019, Soleimani 2020, Houthi 2024, Epic Fury 28 Feb 2026, Hormuz closure 4 Mar 2026). Stacked spread-z money plot. Median + IQR. |
| Trade rule + OOS PnL | 250 | Rule definition, 2010–2018 fit / 2019–present test. Sharpe, drawdown, hit rate, trade count. PnL curve. Honest note on when it fails. |
| Long-term repricing | 200 | Rolling-β chart with three regime overlays. Argument: 2026 is qualitatively different in scale and duration; β/half-life test post-Feb-2026 vs prior. Hormuz risk premium as the mechanism. |
| Limitations | 50 | Event sample size, front-month-only, no execution friction modelled, 2026 regime still unfolding. |

## Required figures (numbered for paper)

1. Spread time series 2000–present, with event dates marked as vertical lines.
2. **Money plot** — stacked spread-z paths across events, days from t=0, with median + IQR band.
3. OOS PnL curve, 2019–present.
4. Rolling 2-year β with three regime overlays.

## Required tables

1. ADF results for log-Brent, log-WTI, residuals (statistic, p-value, conclusion).
2. Per-event metrics — peak |z|, days-to-peak, days-to-renormalisation, pre/post residual variance, pre/post half-life.
3. Backtest summary — trade count, hit rate, mean PnL, OOS Sharpe, max drawdown, average holding period.

## Style notes

- Cut every adjective that isn't load-bearing.
- Every figure earns its caption budget — captions are part of the word count if Onyx counts them.
- Three citations max in the body. Push the rest to footnotes / appendix if the format allows.
- No "we believe" / "we think." State what the data shows.
